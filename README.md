# Dartha-Ops-Global-Electronics-Retailer

# Introduction
This project analyzes the performance of a global electronics retail company operating across multiple continents and managing multiple brands, product categories, and sub-categories. The purpose of the analysis was to identify business and operational gaps affecting the company’s performance.
The company experienced a noticeable decline in revenue and profit in recent years compared to previous peak periods. The objective of this analysis was to investigate potential causes behind this decline by examining key business areas such as product performance, regional sales distribution, delivery timelines, customer demographics, and store performance.

# Result
Through this analysis, insights were generated to help identify weaknesses in operational processes, purchasing patterns, and market performance. The findings provide direction on how the business can improve efficiency, strengthen high-performing markets, optimize product strategy, and address factors that may be limiting overall revenue growth.

# Tools Used
* SQL was used for data cleaning, sorting, filtering, transformation, and calculations. Multiple datasets were integrated and structured in SQL to prepare the data for    analysis and performance evaluation.
* Power BI was used for the final visualization and dashboard presentation to communicate key insights and business recommendations.

# Process Overview
The workflow involved;
1. Data cleaning
2. Data sorting & filtering
3. KPI identification
4. Performance analysis and
5. Dashboard visualization to present the key findings of the project.
